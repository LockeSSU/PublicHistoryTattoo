The History of the Tattoo

Tattoos have a rich history dating back thousands of years. They have been found on mummies in ancient Egypt, etched into the skin of indigenous tribes, and used for various purposes throughout time, from marking rites of passage to signifying social status. Tattoos have evolved from traditional to contemporary art forms and remain a fascinating testament to human expression and culture across different civilizations.
